Top 3 favourite movies of all time according to Adithya.

Fight Club

A cinematic rebellion against conformity raw, relentless, unforgettable.
A gritty descent into chaos and identity, “Fight Club” or should i say "***** ****" unpacks the suppressed rage of modern masculinity and questions the illusion of control in consumer culture.

Kantara

A soul-stirring dive into the heart of coastal Indian heritage.
Rooted in folklore and divine tradition, “Kantara” weaves a breathtaking tale of man, nature, and spirit where faith and fury collide in a cultural spectacle.

ZNMD

A celebration of life, laughter, and letting go.
A poetic road trip of friendship, fear, and freedom “ZNMD” celebrates living in the moment and rediscovering life beyond deadlines.
